Welcome to ComPRePS: Revolutionizing Digital Pathology with AI

ComPRePS (Computational Renal Pathology Suite) is an innovative, cloud-based image analysis tool designed to democratize artificial intelligence (AI) in digital pathology. Developed by a collaborative team led by Dr. Pinaki Sarder at the University of Florida, ComPRePS aims to transform the landscape of histological analysis, particularly in the realm of renal pathology..

Empowering Pathologists with AI

With the proliferation of high-resolution whole-slide brightfield microscopy, ComPRePS leverages cutting-edge deep neural network (DNN)-based AI technologies to revolutionize disease diagnostics and prognostics. By integrating massively parallelized computational power and advanced AI algorithms, ComPRePS enables pathologists to perform complex image analysis tasks with ease.

End-to-End Solutions for Precision Medicine

ComPRePS provides an end-to-end solution for computational renal pathology, offering massive scalability, on-demand cloud computing, and an intuitive web-based user interface. From data upload and storage to slide-level visualization and domain expert interaction, ComPRePS streamlines the entire process, facilitating precision medicine and improving patient care outcomes.

Fostering Collaboration and Innovation

Aligned with the NIH's FAIR Data Principles, ComPRePS fosters collaborative research communities by providing open access to sophisticated AI algorithms and software tools. By democratizing AI-based image analysis, ComPRePS empowers researchers and clinicians worldwide to contribute to more reliable, accurate, and explainable diagnostic methods.
